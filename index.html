News & Events

Stay updated with the latest happenings

September 4, 2025

NACOS DAY 2.0

The annual NACOS Day Event makes a great comeback as NACOS DAY 2.0! Get ready for an unforgettable experience filled with innovation, tech exhibitions, engaging workshops, fun competitions, networking opportunities, and lots of entertainment. It’s not just an event — it’s a celebration of creativity, technology, and community. Don’t miss out!

September 3, 2025

CS Prime Event Final Day

The CS Prime Event is an annual program for Computer Science students, featuring exciting quizzes, inspiring speeches, and innovative software development. The Final Day is the grand highlight — where students showcase and present the software projects they have built, demonstrating their creativity, teamwork, and technical excellence.

September 3, 2025

Second Semester Exams Begins

The long-awaited second semester examinations officially commence. We wish all Computer Science students success as they put their knowledge and hard work to the test. Stay focused, prepare well, and give it your best!
